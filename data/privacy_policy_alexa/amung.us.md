# whos.amung.us - privacy policy

(This statement is only available in our official website language of English) 

**Our Commitment to Your Privacy**   
At whos.amung.us Inc. your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we have provided this notice explaining our online information practices. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where any personally identifiable information (PII) may be requested and/or collected. whos.amung.us Inc. reserves the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time without notice. This privacy policy only affects whos.amung.us and other sites, services, and domains operated by whos.amung.us Inc. it does not pertain in any manner to any websites linking to whos.amung.us (or related domain, operated by whos.amung.us Inc.), or any websites linked to from a whos.amung.us Inc. site or domain. 

whos.amung.us Inc. does not collect any personally identifiable information - except where noted below. 

Data is collected in the following ways: 

  * **Free widget services**   
You will not be required to provide any personally identifiable information, or any information at all, should you choose to install one of our free widgets on your website in order to utilize our statistics services. You, and any user who visits a website containing a whos.amung.us Inc. free widget, will be providing an IP address, page URL reference(s), and browser/operating system information in the request header. This is a standard HTTP request header and is available freely to the operator of any website you choose to visit. We, and/or our partners, may log certain aspects of this header and/or data as part of our services. We, and/or our partners, may log certain aspects of the content where our widgets are installed, as well as non-personally identifiable actions users may take on these sites/pages (such as a copy action). 
  * **Subscription based Pro widget service**   
In order to effectively provide our subscription based Pro service we require owners of websites who wish to use our services provide relevant information for contact and billing purposes only, this may include, but is not limited to: names, addresses, emails, zip/postal code information, phone numbers, billing information, etc. In addition to the information collected by the above free widget service, Pro widgets may collect information from you or any visitor to a site containing a Pro widget, such as user operating system and browser. Again, this information is derived from a standard HTTP request header and is available freely to the operator of any website you choose to visit. We, and/or our partners, may log certain aspects of this header and other information collected via Javascript as part of our services, no personally identifiable information is collected, or released to any third party. We, and/or our partners, may log certain aspects of the content where our widgets are installed, as well as non-personally identifiable actions users may take on these sites/pages (such as a copy action). 
  * **Advertising and all other business relationships**   
Any person, company, or entity choosing to enter into an advertising and/or other business relationship with whos.amung.us Inc. will be required to provide any necessary personally identifiable information in order to complete the business transaction/relationship. This information may include, but is not limited to: names, addresses, phone numbers, server information, email addresses, trademarks and copyrights used in and advertising capacity.

**IP Address Usage**   
Every computer and device connected to the Internet is assigned a unique number known as an Internet protocol (IP) address. Since these numbers are usually assigned in country-based blocks, an IP address can often be used to identify the country, region and city from which a computer is connecting to the Internet. Like other web traffic analytic service providers, whos.amung.us collects the IP address of website visitors in order to provide website owners with a general idea of where in the world their visitors come from, this is known as geolocation. Regardless of a website owners usage of whos.amung.us, or other analytic services, they have access to IP addresses for visitors to their website(s). We anonymize IP addresses in our systems by zeroing the last octet out. 

**Cookies, Web Beacons & Partners**   
whos.amung.us Inc. may place cookies on your system in order to provide a better and more informative service to our users. A cookie is simply a small text file stored by your browser that is sent by your browser to the corresponding site that originally set the cookie (ie. cookies are only available to the site that originally set them). These cookies may include, but are not limited to, user preference information and unique user IDs for internal tracking purposes. No personally identifiable information (PII) will be contained in these cookies. 

Some of our partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site, products, or services. Our partners include, but are not limited to, Google. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, telephone number, or any other personally identifiable information) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about these practices, used by Google, or to opt out of having cookies set on your system, please visit [Google's Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html) page. 

These third-party partners, ad servers, or ad networks may power the services, advertisements, and links that appear, or are used on whos.amung.us and any of its services. Because your web browser must request this data from the third party’s servers, those companies can view, edit or set their own cookies, just as if you had requested a web page from their website directly. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs, but no personally identifiable information is transmitted. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party partners to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see. 

whos.amung.us Inc. has no access to or control over these cookies or other technology that are used by third-party advertisers or partners.
